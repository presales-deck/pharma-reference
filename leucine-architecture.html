Leucine: Integrated

Every agent action passes through MCP and is mediated by the ontology. Agents never query source systems directly. All reads and writes create a single, traceable record of every AI action taken on the platform.

Principle 01
MCP End-to-End
One standardized protocol from source systems through the ontology to agent actions. No translation layers, no middleware gaps.
Principle 02
Agent-Ready Database
Structured process data and unstructured paper records in one unified layer. The agent accesses everything the way your QA team thinks about it.
Principle 03
Skills, Not Prompts
Engineered procedures with defined inputs, outputs, and guardrails at every step. Refined across pharma companies, adapted to your process.
